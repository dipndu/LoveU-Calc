Our Origin Story

The internet is full of broken tools.

Before LoveU Calc, finding a simple answer online was a frustrating experience. You would search for a calculator, click a link, and be bombarded by pop-up ads, slow loading times, and confusing layouts. You came for a number, but you left with a headache.

We decided to build something different. We asked ourselves: "What if a calculator website felt like a modern app? What if it respected the user's time and privacy?"

And so, LoveU Calc was born. A suite of tools built by engineers and designers who believe that even a simple math problem deserves a beautiful solution.
